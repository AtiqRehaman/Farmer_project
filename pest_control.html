Pest Control Methods

Chemical Control

Chemical control involves the use of pesticides to manage pest populations. This method is effective but requires careful application to minimize environmental impact.

Biological control uses natural predators or parasites to manage pest populations. This eco-friendly method promotes biodiversity and reduces chemical use.
