Farming Tips

Tip 1: Crop Rotation

Rotate your crops each season to maintain soil health and reduce pests.

Tip 2: Soil Testing

Regularly test your soil to understand its nutrient levels and pH balance.

Tip 3: Water Management

Implement efficient irrigation systems to conserve water and improve crop yield.

Tip 4: Pest Control

Use integrated pest management techniques to minimize chemical use and protect crops.
